## Why we built OpesFlux

For years, businesses around the world have been forced to choose between software built for one market and software that tries to do everything but does nothing well.

A retailer in Dubai runs their POS on one system, their accounting on another, and their payroll on a third — none of which talk to each other properly. A restaurant group in Manchester reconciles sales manually every evening because their EPOS doesn't connect to their accounting package. A wholesaler in Karachi tracks purchase orders in spreadsheets because no affordable system handles their local tax rules.

We built OpesFlux to solve all of this. One platform. Every feature. Any country.

## What OpesFlux does

OpesFlux is an ERP and POS system that covers every operational need a growing business has:

- **Point of Sale** — touch, barcode, offline mode, split payments, gift cards
- **Inventory management** — real-time stock across unlimited locations
- **Accounting & finance** — double-entry, bank reconciliation, multi-currency
- **Payroll & HR** — jurisdiction-configurable deductions, timesheets, leave
- **CRM & customers** — profiles, loyalty, credit limits, aged receivables
- **Reports & analytics** — P&L, cash flow, sales by location, staff performance

Everything shares one database. One login. One source of truth.

## Who it's for

OpesFlux is built for businesses from 1 to 500+ staff across six industries:

- Retail (shops, chains, market stalls)
- Restaurants & hospitality
- Wholesale & distribution
- Construction
- Manufacturing
- Professional services

It works in any country. Tax rates, payroll rules, and reporting formats are all configurable per jurisdiction — UK, Pakistan, UAE, India, USA, Australia, and more.

## What makes it different

Most ERP software is either too expensive, too complicated, or built for one country. OpesFlux is modular (pay for what you need), web-based (no installation), and genuinely global (no hardcoded tax rules).
