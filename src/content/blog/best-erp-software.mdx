Finding the right ERP system is one of the most important decisions a growing business can make. With dozens of options on the market, knowing where to start can feel overwhelming.

## What to look for in ERP software

The best ERP software for your business depends on your industry, team size, and the markets you operate in. Here are the key things to evaluate:

**Coverage** — Does it handle your core workflows? At minimum, look for POS, inventory, accounting, and payroll in a single platform. Switching between tools creates gaps and costs time.

**Compliance** — If you operate across jurisdictions, your ERP needs to handle local tax rules automatically. Manually configuring tax rates is a maintenance burden that only grows as you scale.

**Scalability** — Start with what you need now, but make sure the platform can grow with you. Adding locations, users, or currencies shouldn't require a platform migration.

**Support** — ERP implementations fail more often from lack of support than from bad software. Prioritise vendors who offer onboarding, training, and responsive support.
